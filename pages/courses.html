Flexible learning

Our 600-hour, 6-month-long courses are flexible, convienient, and include all state-required cirriculum, combining book work and hands-on practical performances. We utilize both online and in-person resources to keep your esthetics journey practical and enjoyable.

Esthetics

Our Esthetics Program is designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to perform a variety of beauty treatments, including skincare, facials, waxing, makeup application, and more. You will learn the technical aspects of esthetics, while also developing your customer service and professional communication skills.

  • Milady Textbook
  • CIMA online learning platform
  • Mannequin head

  • State board kit
