The DuoGlo Difference

Our founders, Mimi and Kaelie, saw a severe lack of comprehensive education in their beloved field of esthetics, and set out to make a difference. At DuoGlo Esethetics training, our goal is to prepare you not only for your state board exams, but for a lasting and fulfilling career in esthetics.
