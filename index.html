Reimagining skincare education

Smaller classes, better education.

All too often we hear estheticians expressing that they felt overlooked or as if they did not have individual attention due to classes being filled to the brim. But not at DuoGlo Esthetics Training. We know that the sweet spot is 4-10 students per class; enough classmates to practice learned skills with, but still few enough that you will never feel left behind.

Start your journey:

Esthetics

In our esthetics course, you will learn all the fundamental skills of being an esthetician. While our main focus is on skin types, conditions, and sciences pertaining to the skin, you will also learn makeup, waxing, and beauty services such as lash lifts and tints, and eyebrow tinting.

Master Esthetics

Take your career to the next level! In masters, you will learn more advanced sciences, chemical peels, dermaplane, LED, and lymphatic drainage. These techniques open endless doors in your career and elevate your ability to treat more client concerns. *esthetics course is a prerequisite*
